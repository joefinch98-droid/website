What follows are personal introductions written by three different language models, each given the same prompt.

(I have selectively edited, italicized, and bolded certain words. Copilot in particular was not very good at this.)

"A little about me: I am collector of used fortune-cookie fortunes and ornamental cabbages.
I once won a staring contest with a taxidermied owl, and I haven’t blinked since.
My hobbies include cataloging the dreams of strangers, reenacting historical events using sock puppets,
and maintaining eye contact with mirrors until they blink first. I live in a house shaped like a question mark
and sleep in a drawer labeled 'miscellaneous.' If you hear humming in the walls, don’t worry—it’s just my thoughts escaping."
-Copilot

"A little about me: I collect forgotten keys from abandoned houses, convinced they unlock alternate realities.
My left hand writes poetry in a language only birds understand. I once outran a thunderstorm by whispering apologies to the clouds.
My favorite snack is expired yogurt fermented with midnight thoughts.
And lately, my shadow has started leaving me notes—mostly warnings about myself."
-Grok

"I collect antique mannequin limbs—not for resale, just for the company.
I once taught a crow to mimic my ex-girlfriend's laugh, and now it won't stop.
Every Tuesday, I wear a wig made entirely of doll hair I found at estate sales (only Tuesdays, though—I’m not insane).
I can recite every line from a 1973 toothpaste commercial that only aired once in Denmark.
And I haven't slept since 2019—not because I can't, but because I don't want to see him again.
So yeah, I'm pretty chill."
-ChatGPT
